1.0 Installing Oynk:

  1. Click on the ”App Store” app from your device.
  2. Search for “Oynk” from the App Store.
  3. Click “Install”.
  4. Click the button marked as Accept.
  5. Oynk will be automatically installed in your device. 

1.1 Registering with Oynk:

  1. Open Oynk app in your phone.
  2. Click on Agree in Terms & Conditions.
  3. Enter your registered mobile number.
  4. Enter your Name.
  5. Enter the 4 digit password/ PIN number. 
  6. On clicking OTP field, an SMS with OTP number will be sent to your registered mobile number.
  7. Please enter the six digit OTP number on your application to activate your Oynk account.
  8. Press Submit button.

2. How to pay a Merchant?:

  1. Open Oynk app in your device. 
  2. Home screen of Oynk will appear.
  3. Click on “Pay a Merchant” on Home Screen.
  4. Enter the 5 digit Merchant Code to whom you want to transfer money. (Note: Retailer discloses you the Merchant Code).
  5. Enter the Amount to be transferred.
  6. Click on Pay button.
  7. Enter the 4-digit PIN number and Click on Proceed button.
  8. It displays “Transaction Successful” on your screen.
  9. Money will be successfully transferred to merchant account.

2.1 Receiving money from merchant? (For Un-Registered Users) :

  1. Tell your mobile number to the merchant.
  2. If merchant transfers the amount, an SMS with the message “Your wallet has been credited with the amount and a link” will be sent to your mobile number.
  3. Click on the Link displaying in the SMS.
  4. Download & Install Oynk (As mentioned in Step 1.0). 
  5. Register with Oynk (As mentioned in Step 1.1). 
  6. Money will be successfully added to your wallet.

2.2 While receiving change from merchant? (For Registered Users):

  1. Tell your mobile number to the merchant.
  2. If merchant pays the amount, it displays a notification “Transaction Successful” on your screen.
  3. Open Oynk Wallet.
  4. Money will be successfully added into your account and balance will be auto-updated.

3. Recharging your mobile:

  1. Open Oynk app in your phone.
  2. Click on “Recharge” Option on Home Screen.
  3. Select the mobile operator of your number.
  4. Enter the mobile number to recharge.
  5. Enter the Recharge Amount from the available plans of your corresponding network.
  6. Click on Recharge button.
  7. Enter your 4-digit passcode and click on Proceed.
  8. Recharge will be successfully done to the entered number.

4. Sending money to your friends:

  1. Open Oynk app in your phone.
  2. Click on “Send Money” Option on Home Screen.
  3. Enter your friend number or select number from your contact list.
  4. Enter the amount to share with your friend and click on Send Button.
  5. Enter your 4-digit passcode.
  6. It displays “Money Transferred” on your screen.
  7. Money will be successfully transferred to your friend.

5. Change Your Passcode:

  1. Open Oynk app in your phone.
  2. Press Menu button on top left corner of screen.
  3. Select “My Account” Option from the Menu.
  4. Click on “Change Passcode” Option.
  5. Enter your 4-digit old passcode.
  6. Now enter your 4-digit new passcode twice.
  7. It displays “Passcode Changed Successfully” on your screen.
  8. Passcode will be successfully changed in your app.

6. How to view recent transactions?:

  1. Open Oynk app in your phone.
  2. In the bottom of the Home Screen appears, “RECENT TRANSACTIONS”, Swipe it up.
  3. Completed Transactions displays Successful Debit and Credit Transactions.
  4. Pending Transactions displays the transactions that are not approved by the merchant. 
  5. Credit transactions are marked by i.e. money transferred from merchant to your account.
  6. Debit transactions are marked by i.e. money transferred from your account to merchant

7. In Case of Queries/ Support/ Feedback?:

  1. Open Oynk app in your phone.
  2. Press Menu button on top left corner of screen.
  3. Select Contact Us from the Menu.
  4. Click on the mobile number and ask your queries and suggest your feedbacks (24*7 Customer Support).
  5. Or click on the e-mail support@oynk.in and ask your queries or share your views and feedback to us.
