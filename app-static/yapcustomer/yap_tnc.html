Definitions


1.    Company or Corporate - A company is an association or collection of individuals sharing as common purpose and unite in order to focus their various talents and organize their collectively available skills or resources to achieve specific, declared goals

2.    Merchant Establishment - Is a place where customer visits for buying goods or services, the person offering such good or service is called the merchant and the establishment is the place where such good or service is offered.

3.    Point of Sale (POS) - Is the time and place where a retail transaction is completed. It is the point at which a customer makes a payment to the merchant in exchange for goods or after provision of a service.

4.    Mobile Application or Mobile App - A mobile application, most commonly referred to as an app, is a type of application software designed to run on a mobile device, such as a smartphone or tablet computer. Mobile applications frequently serve to provide users with similar services to those accessed on PCs.

5.    Quick Response (QR) Code - A QR code (quick response code) is a type of 2D bar code that is used to provide easy access to information through a smartphone. In this process, known as mobile tagging, the smartphone's owner points the phone at a QR code and opens a barcode reader app which works in conjunction with the phone's camera.

Product

The DCB YAP Employee Benefits card is a Visa Prepaid card that has been issued to employees of corporates based on an application for issuance of the card by the employee or their employers. The card comes with multiple pockets with capability to issue various allowances including but not limited to Meal, Medical, Fuel and Corporate travel and Entertainment reimbursements as provided by the employer from time to time.

The DCB YAP Employee Benefits card also comes with a linked Mobile application that can be downloaded from respective mobile platforms for use in conjunction with the card. The mobile application allows the user to pay at merchant establishments by scanning a Quick Response Code and pay using the mVisa service provided by Visa to pay at retail stores. The employee will authorize the transaction using a Personal Identification Number (PIN) selected by the employee at the time of registering with the application. The application will also be upgraded with features from time to time and the Bank shall communicate the same to the employee. Additionally, the employee can review last few transactions, check balance and lock or unlock the card using the mobile application.

The DCB YAP Employee Benefits card comes with multiple pockets to meet different benefit requirements. Each pocket will have appropriate usage limit and restrictions, for example, the meal allowance can be used only for spends on ready to eat food and beverages. The fuel allowance can be used only for refilling fuel at Petrol pumps. Please refer to the detailed FAQ section for more on the usage of the different pockets
