Transaction Flow

The advent of Neo-Banking accounts

  • The customer enters their details along with KYC information in the Neo-banking app
  • M2P authenticates those KYC details at the backend by using the bank's API
  • On successful verification, an account is created for the customer
  • Neo-bank informs the customer about the account opening and shares the details
  • The customer will then proceed to fund the account through normal channels of banking transfer
  • The neo-banking savings account is now fully operational
