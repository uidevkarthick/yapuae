Here's how we spliced SlicePay's money flow

the Challenge

No Credit history = No loan

Getting a credit line from established and traditional lenders is not a choice for individuals with no credit history. Yet, it was an untapped stream of potential borrowers with infinite possibilities.

the solution

No credit history = SlicePay

Slice, a fintech, was designing a credit platform to serve those potential borrowers often ignored by traditional lenders. It offered a line of credit based on the applicant's background information so that borrowers could get loans and, in turn, build their credit histories.

end point overview

Workings of YAP credit platform

YAP acts as a payment layer facilitating transactions between network and client

  • Slice pay card is swiped at the shop's terminal
  • The network reroutes the transaction to YAP, where the card details are authenticated
  • Then, in real-time, YAP sends a request to Slice for transaction approval
  • The transaction is then confirmed and the account to be debited is also confirmed
  • The money is moved from the designated account to the card, and the transaction goes through
