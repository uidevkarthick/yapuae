the Challenge

How to build a credit score?

Getting a credit card is not an option for individuals with no credit history. The same applies to individuals who have a history of bad credit. However, this stream of borrowers presented a goldmine of opportunities.

the solution

Get a secured credit card using FD and build your credit score

Lender offered a secured credit card against Fixed deposits opened by individuals with no or bad credit score. It opened up a flow of new opportunities and created new markets for secured borrowing.

end point overview

Workings of YAP Credit

The journey of issuing a secured credit card

  • The customer opens the Fixed deposit along with Aadhaar-based XML verification
  • The funds are transferred to the partner bank, and YAP is notified of the transfer
  • Bank cross-checks the transfer and issues a fixed deposit certificate
  • Once the certificate is issued, YAP proceeds to assign the credit card to the customer
  • Lender is notified to assign limit, and the credit card is issued to the customer
