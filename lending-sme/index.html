Transaction Flow

How does our SME credit platform work?

  • M2P facilitates transactions between network and client
  • The client’s card is swiped at the point-of-sale
  • The system reroutes the transaction to M2P, where the card details are authenticated
  • Then, in real-time, M2P sends a request to the client for transaction approval
  • The transaction is then confirmed, and the account to be debited is also confirmed
  • The money is moved from the designated account to the card
  • The transaction is completed
