Transaction Flow

Experience M2P’s Payment Card Journey

  • The prepaid, credit, or debit card is swiped at a Point-of-Sale or ATM
  • The network routes the transaction to M2P for authorization
  • Based on the set rules, the card details will be authenticated
  • Verifies whether the transaction could be approved
  • Optionally, if the partner prefers to approve the transaction, approval is sought
  • On receiving a success response from the partner M2P finally authorizes the transaction
  • The account to be debited is confirmed, and fund transfer is initiated
  • The transaction is completed successfully
