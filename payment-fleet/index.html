Transaction Flow

How our APIs power the fleet cards?

  • A virtual account is assigned to a fleet management company, bridged to a fleet card
  • The customer loads the virtual account through regular fund transfer channels
  • The virtual account, in turn, will credit the fleet card for the journey
  • The supplier or driver can use the fleet card for the journey’s expenses
  • In case of low balance, the virtual account can be reloaded
