end point overview

Get paid as you desire using APIs

Delve into the mechanisms of UPI transactions as they flow through YAP

  • The customer goes to the checkout page after choosing the product
  • They are exposed to the UPI payment option
  • The customer enters their VPA ID and clicks the “PAY” option
  • The transaction is continued in the UPI app, with the customer receiving the collect request
  • The customer then opens the merchant’s tab and accepts the collect request
  • Customer then proceeds to enter their UPI PIN to make the payment
  • The PIN is accepted, and their account is subsequently debited for the purchase
  • The merchant’s account is then credited with the requisite funds
  • The successful transaction status is generated, resulting in the purchase of the product
